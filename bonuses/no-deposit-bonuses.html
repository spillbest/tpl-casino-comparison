Types of No-Deposit Bonuses

Free spins

A fixed number of spins on selected slots at a fixed stake; winnings convert to bonus funds.

Bonus cash

A small bonus balance (typically €5–10) usable across eligible games with weighting rules.

Free play time

Rare format: a timed session with starting credits; only the net result above a threshold converts.

Conditions for Claiming and Using the Bonus

  • Account verification — identity confirmation is required before any withdrawal.
  • One per person/household — duplicate accounts void winnings under the terms.
  • Game restrictions — selected slots only; table games often excluded or weighted at 10%.
  • Maximum bet while wagering — typically €1–5 per spin; exceeding it can void the bonus.
  • Win cap — convertible winnings are limited, commonly €25–100.
