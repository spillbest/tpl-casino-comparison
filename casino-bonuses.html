Understanding Wagering Requirements

Placeholder worked example: a €100 bonus at x30 wagering requires €3,000 in qualifying bets before withdrawal. With slots at ~96% RTP, the expected cost of completing that turnover is around €120 — often more than the bonus itself.

  • Multiplier basis — bonus only vs deposit + bonus (doubles the turnover).
  • Game weighting — slots usually 100%, table games 5–10%, live often 0%.
  • Validity window — 7 days vs 30 days changes feasibility entirely.
  • Max bet rule — exceeding it can void the bonus and winnings.
